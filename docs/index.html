But every journey has its plot twists, and mine is filled with algorithms and ambition.

My passion lies in developing intelligent systems that make a real-world impact. Whether I'm crafting AI models or strategizing in a chess game, I love playing with algorithms.

Ever wondered why we don't use light as a backup communication method in space for astronauts and rovers? Or how tweaks in human-machine interfaces could end pandemics or prevent future ones? These intriguing questions fuel my curiosity and drive my work.
